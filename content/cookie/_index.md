## Summary

**This site uses only strictly necessary technical cookies.** No profiling, analytics, marketing or third-party tracking cookies are present. For this reason **no consent banner is shown**, in compliance with the [Italian DPA guidelines of 10 June 2021](https://www.garanteprivacy.it/web/guest/home/docweb/-/docweb-display/docweb/9677876).

Strictly necessary technical cookies do not require user consent pursuant to art. 122 of the Italian Privacy Code (D.Lgs. 196/2003 as amended by D.Lgs. 101/2018) and art. 5(3) of Directive 2002/58/EC (ePrivacy Directive).

## Cookies used

| Cookie | Type | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `__cf_bm` | Strictly necessary | Distinguishes human from automated traffic (bot management) for site security and delivery | 30 minutes | Cloudflare, Inc. (CDN/edge security) |

The `__cf_bm` cookie is set directly by Cloudflare in its role as data processor for perimeter security purposes. Further details: [Cloudflare cookie policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/).

## Analytics and tracking tools

This site **does not use** Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, social plugins, fingerprinting, advertising networks or other profiling tools. Access statistics, where required, are derived solely from technical CDN logs retained for security purposes as set out in the [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).

## How to manage cookies

Strictly necessary technical cookies do not require consent. You can remove them via your browser settings. Disabling technical cookies may affect site usability.

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/Cookies)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge)
