About resysto

resysto was created to help organizations move from ad-hoc security work to a programmatic approach. Too many businesses—especially small and medium-sized enterprises—struggle with cybersecurity because they lack the structure, resources, or dedicated expertise to build comprehensive security programs.

Our Mission

To democratize enterprise-grade cybersecurity management by providing organizations of all sizes with the tools, frameworks, and intelligence they need to build resilient security programs—without requiring a full-time CISO or massive budgets.

Developed by Agora Security

resysto is built and maintained by Agora Security, a cybersecurity consulting and technology company based in Italy. We work with organizations across Europe to design, implement, and manage comprehensive security programs.
